Why Choose Aced It!

Aced It!

Test Academy provides lessons in SAT Prep by elite test prep trainer Richard L Davis: a Duke graduate and National Merit Scholar who personally scored in the 99th percentile of the SAT by age 16. Your child will learn from Head Instructor Davis while working from real SATs and receiving interactive instruction responsive to each class’s unique needs. Each lesson has a length of 2½ hours, which keeps meeting times manageably short, and with an overall schedule of 8 weeks to complete the course, students have time to digest the material rather than cram it. With assigned weekly homework and the option of Homework Monitoring and Reporting, Aced It! Test Academy helps parents, students and the instructor stay together on the same page. We even offer a Score Boost Guarantee to show that we mean it!

Choose wisely!

You can choose a Bronze, Silver, Gold or Platinum plan depending on your child’s needs and your situation. Bronze plans grant enrollment and full access to an 18-hour live class for a $199, a fraction of what live SAT courses usually cost. Or enroll your child in a Silver Plan with Homework Monitoring and a Score Boost Guarantee, a Gold Small Group Focus Class with access to our Tutor Team, or even a Platinum Completely Individual Course to ensure that your child receives the best education available. In short, our pricing at Aced It! beats the competition, and so does our quality. The team at Aced It! believes in the ability and the value of every individual student. We believe it, and we care. Join us – it’s time for your child to work hard, get expert help and discover what success feels like.
